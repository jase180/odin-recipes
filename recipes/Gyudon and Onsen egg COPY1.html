Gyudon and Onsen egg

Description

If you ever had a Yoshinoya beef bowl, you would be craving it once in a while. When there isn't a Yoshinoya nearby, your only resort is to make it yourself.

Luckily it isn't too hard. The tender beef with just the right amount of fat, the soft onion that provides a soft crunch and that decadent onsen egg giving richness all on top of a soft bed of white rice. Mmmmmmmmm

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Onion
  • 500g Shabu shabu Beef slices
  • 1/2 cupWhite rice
  • 1 Egg
  • Scallions (optional)

Steps

  1. Cut onion into long strips with preferred thickness
  2. Make sauce and put into small pan
  3. Put onion and cook until onion is preferred softness
  4. Put beef in
  5. Make Onsen egg
  6. Put everything on top of rice bowl and scatter scallions
